DietShopper: Project Presentation

DietShopper was born out of a personal motivation to put my knowledge into practice. As an aspiring back end developer with a passion for healthy living, I noticed a gap in the market for a tool that could make dietary adherence easier and more convenient. DietShopper is the result of my efforts to bridge this gap, providing a solution that integrates personalized shopping lists, recipes, and dietary recommendations.

Technology Stack:

  • Front-End: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Back-End: Python
  • Database: SQLite
